About Guardian Of the Rose

Welcome to Guardian of the Rose. Here's what you wanna know About Us:

We have started establishing this hotline/website since 2018. Our Website has been operating with 6 people from the beginning, and has faced numerous support from the people.

800+ supporters

Welcome to our domestic hotline, founded in 2018 with the aim of providing support and assistance to those affected by domestic violence and abuse. At our hotline, we understand that domestic violence and abuse can have a devastating impact on individuals, families, and communities. Our mission is to provide a safe and confidential space for victims and survivors to access the support and resources they need to break the cycle of abuse and start on the path towards healing and recovery.

Lifetime Support

Our team consists of dedicated and trained professionals who are passionate about making a difference in the lives of those affected by domestic violence and abuse. We provide a range of services, including crisis intervention, safety planning, emotional support, referrals to community resources, and education on healthy relationships.
