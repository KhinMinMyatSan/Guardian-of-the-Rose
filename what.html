How can we be safe from violence?

In stressful times, you can find peace by talking to people who support you. Share your situation with others so that you can gain perspective, create plans, and feel comforted. This supportive community can also help you rehearse escape routes and store extra money, clothing, or car keys if you must escape quickly in the future. What we can offer ?

Do you need help with crisis support?

The website can offer crisis support services to victims of domestic violence. This can include a 24-hour helpline, live chat support, or email support.

Do you need help with information and education?

The website can provide comprehensive information and resources on domestic violence, including the types of abuse, signs of abuse, cycle of abuse, legal remedies, and safety planning.

Are you in need of safety planning?

The website can provide information on how to develop a safety plan for victims and their families. A safety plan can help victims prepare for emergencies and protect themselves from further abuse.

Do you want to get legal advice?

The website can provide information on legal remedies for victims, such as restraining orders, divorce, and custody arrangements. The website can also offer referrals to legal services.

Do you need help with Counseling and Therapy?

The website can offer information on counseling and therapy services for victims and abusers. This can include online counseling services, support groups, and other resources.
